About Light Composites

Located in Poway, California, Light Composite is a well known aerospace and medical supplier that employs approximately 20 people and is principally specialized in design, development, manufacture of advanced composite parts.

Our Mission

We design customer application, build complex composites parts and deliver innovative solutions to help our customers making systems that fly and save people life.

Our Vision

Be a very long term reliable partner for our customers, design to their need, achieved great and chalenging projects.
